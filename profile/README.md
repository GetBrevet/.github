Real exam questions, hands-on labs, and verified credentials. Every question is written by people who have taken the exam, calibrated against the real blueprint, and verified against an official doc.

## Principles

**Earned, not gamified.** Streaks shown as plain numbers. No fireworks, no loss framing, no freeze paywall. No leaderboards.

**Every question is real-exam-grade.** Tagged with cert, domain, subdomain, difficulty, and services. Every distractor is a real service, wrong for an articulable reason. Every question carries a verified-against source URL pointing to an official doc.

**Current services only.** SOA-C02 → SOA-C03. OAI → OAC. WAF Classic → WAFv2. Retired services don't appear as correct answers.

**Direct, respectful of your time.** No "crush it" energy. Credentials are *earned* and *verified* — never *achieved*, *unlocked*, or *won*.
